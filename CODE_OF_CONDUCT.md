# Code Of Conduct

Mana contributors are expected to work respectfully, focus on evidence, and keep
human accountability explicit.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Critique designs, evidence, and risks rather than people.
- Treat AI-generated findings as review inputs, not as personal judgments.
- Make approval gates and ownership clear when discussing delivery risk.
- Protect confidential, customer, production, and credential data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, secrets, credentials, or unredacted production logs.
- Using AI output to bypass accountable human review.
- Misrepresenting speculative AI findings as verified facts.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close discussions, reject contributions,
or restrict participation when behavior undermines safety, confidentiality, or
productive collaboration.
